About Deepak Dog Kennel

Deepak Dog Kennel & Pet Shop is Ambala Cantt's most trusted pet care destination, proudly serving pet parents since 7th February 2010. We are dedicated to providing healthy, well-bred puppies and comprehensive pet care services.

Our facility offers premium quality dog and cat food from top international brands, professional pet grooming services, a safe and hygienic dog hostel, and a wide range of pet accessories and supplies. We serve the entire Tricity area including Ambala, Chandigarh, Panchkula, Mohali, and Zirakpur.

At Deepak Dog Kennel, every pet receives individual attention, love, and professional care. Our team of experienced handlers and groomers ensure your furry friends are happy, healthy, and well-groomed.
