❓ Frequently Asked Questions ❓

🐕 What is the price range for puppies?
Puppy prices start from ₹15,000 to ₹50,000 depending on breed, age, and vaccination status. Shih Tzu (₹20,000-35,000), Labrador (₹15,000-25,000), Pomeranian (₹18,000-30,000), Husky (₹35,000-50,000). Call us for current availability and prices!
✂️ Do you offer home delivery for pet food?
Yes! We offer free home delivery for pet food and accessories within Ambala Cantt. For nearby areas like Chandigarh, Panchkula, and Zirakpur, minimal delivery charges apply. Minimum order ₹500 for free delivery.
🏠 Is the dog hostel safe and clean?
Absolutely! Our dog hostel has 24/7 CCTV surveillance, clean individual kennels, regular feeding schedule, daily exercise time, and trained caretakers. We also provide regular photo/video updates to owners. Just ₹300/day with food included.
💉 Are the puppies vaccinated?
Yes! All our puppies come with complete vaccination records, deworming history, and health certificates. We ensure every puppy is healthy before adoption. We also provide first free vet consultation.
🕐 What are your opening hours?
We are open 7 days a week from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Sunday also open! You can visit anytime or call us for appointments. For emergencies, we are available 24/7 on phone.
